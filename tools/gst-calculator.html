Formulas

How GST is Calculated

Two modes — add GST to a base price, or extract GST from an inclusive price.

➕ Adding GST

GST Exclusive → Inclusive

GST Amount = Price × Rate / 100
Total Price = Price + GST Amount

CGST = SGST = GST Amount / 2
IGST = GST Amount (full)

Use this when your quoted price doesn't include GST and you need to add it on top.

➖ Removing GST

GST Inclusive → Exclusive

GST Amount = Price × Rate / (100 + Rate)
Original Price = Price − GST Amount

CGST = SGST = GST Amount / 2
IGST = GST Amount (full)

Use this when a given price already includes GST and you need the original base price.

📖 Worked Example — 18% GST on ₹10,000

1
Original Price (exclusive of GST) = ₹10,000
2
GST Amount = 10,000 × 18 / 100 = ₹1,800
3
For Intra-State: CGST = ₹900 (9%) + SGST = ₹900 (9%) = ₹1,800
4
Total Invoice Amount = 10,000 + 1,800 = ₹11,800
5
For Inter-State: IGST = ₹1,800 (full 18%, no split)

Frequently Asked Questions

For GST exclusive (adding GST): GST Amount = Original Price × GST Rate / 100. Total Price = Original Price + GST Amount. For GST inclusive (removing GST): GST Amount = Total Price × GST Rate / (100 + GST Rate). Original Price = Total Price − GST Amount.
India has five GST slabs: 0% (essential items like fresh produce), 5% (household necessities like packaged food), 12% (standard goods like medicines and ghee), 18% (most services and consumer goods), and 28% (luxury items like premium cars and tobacco). Most services fall under 18%.
CGST (Central GST) and SGST (State GST) are levied on intra-state (within the same state) transactions — each at half the total GST rate. For example, at 18%, CGST = 9% and SGST = 9%. IGST (Integrated GST) at the full rate applies to inter-state transactions and imports — no splitting.
To extract GST from a GST-inclusive price, use the formula: GST Amount = (Inclusive Price × GST Rate) / (100 + GST Rate). For example, if the total price is ₹11,800 at 18% GST: GST = (11,800 × 18) / 118 = ₹1,800. Original price = ₹10,000. Use the "Remove GST" mode in our calculator above.
