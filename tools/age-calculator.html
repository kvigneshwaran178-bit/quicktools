Free Age Calculator Online — Calculate Exact Age from Date of Birth

Our free age calculator online gives you your precise age in years, months, days, hours, and even minutes — just by entering your date of birth. Whether you need to calculate age from date of birth for a form, a medical record, a legal document, or just out of curiosity, this tool handles it all instantly and for free.

How to Calculate Age from Date of Birth

The formula is straightforward: subtract your birth date from today's date (or any reference date), accounting for how many days are in each month and whether leap years are involved. Our calculator handles all this complexity automatically so you get accurate results every time.

  • Enter your date of birth in the field above
  • Optionally choose a custom reference date (default is today)
  • Click Calculate My Age to see your age in years, months, and days
  • Scroll down to see total days lived, weeks, hours, and days to your next birthday

Age Calculator India — Why It Matters

In India, knowing your exact age is important for many official purposes — from government schemes like senior citizen concessions at age 60, to UPSC eligibility (typically up to age 32), to checking if you're eligible for the National Pension System. Our age calculator India-optimized tool uses the Gregorian calendar which is standard across all Indian legal and official documents.

💡 Leap Year Handling: Our calculator correctly accounts for leap years (Feb 29). If you're born on February 29, your birthday in non-leap years is counted as March 1.

Age Difference Calculator

Need to find the age gap between two people? Use the Age Difference tab. This is handy for couples, siblings, parent-child comparisons, or any situation where you need to know the exact difference in years, months, and days between two birthdates.

Future Age Calculator

Curious how old you'll be on a specific date in the future? The Future Age tab lets you pick any future date and instantly shows your age on that day — great for retirement planning, milestone events, or just knowing how old you'll be when your favourite movie releases!
