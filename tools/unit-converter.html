Conversion Formulas

Common formulas used by our converter for accurate, instant results.

cm → inches
inches = cm × 0.393701
km → miles
miles = km × 0.621371
kg → lbs
lbs = kg × 2.20462
°C → °F
°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32
°C → K
K = °C + 273.15
km/h → mph
mph = km/h × 0.621371
m² → ft²
ft² = m² × 10.7639
liters → gallons
gal = liters × 0.264172
feet → meters
m = feet × 0.3048
