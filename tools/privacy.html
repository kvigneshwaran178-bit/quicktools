📋 Overview

QuickTools ("we", "our", or "the site") is a free online calculator platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at quicktools.in.

The short version: we collect virtually no personal data. All tool calculations happen entirely in your browser and are never transmitted to us.

📊 Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data such as your name, email, address, or payment information. We do not require you to create an account.

We use Google Analytics (via gtag.js) to collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic region (country/city level)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Referral source (how you found us)

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally. It helps us understand which tools are most useful and how to improve the site.

🧮 Calculator Data

All values you enter into QuickTools calculators (e.g. your height, weight, loan amount, income) are processed entirely within your browser. This data is never sent to our servers and is discarded when you close or refresh the page.

We have no access to any inputs you enter into our tools.

🍪 Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Dark mode preference — stored in localStorage on your device to remember your theme choice. Not transmitted anywhere.
  • Google Analytics cookies — anonymous cookies set by Google Analytics to track aggregate site usage. You can opt out via Google Analytics Opt-out.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party marketing cookies.

🤝 Third-Party Services

QuickTools uses two external services:

  • Google Analytics — for anonymous usage statistics. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — for web typography. May log your IP address per Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with advertisers or other third parties.

👤 Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions or concerns about privacy on QuickTools, you can contact us at any time.

If you wish to opt out of Google Analytics tracking, use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about privacy?

We're happy to answer any privacy-related questions. Reach out anytime.
