Writing Tips

10 Tips to Write Cleaner, Stronger Text

01

Cut Filler Words

Remove "very," "really," "just," and "quite." These add words without adding meaning.

02

Aim for One Idea Per Sentence

Long sentences confuse readers. Break complex thoughts into shorter, clearer ones.

03

Use Active Voice

"The dog bit the man" beats "The man was bitten by the dog." Active is shorter and stronger.

04

Vary Sentence Length

Mix short punchy sentences with longer ones to create rhythm and keep readers engaged.

05

Watch Keyword Density

For SEO, keep your primary keyword at 1–2% of total words. Over-stuffing hurts rankings.

06

Strong First Sentences

Readers decide in seconds. Start paragraphs with your most important point, not build-up.

07

Use the Flesch Score

Aim for grade 8–9 reading level for most web content. Short words and sentences help.

08

Break Up Paragraphs

Online readers scan. Keep paragraphs to 3–4 sentences max for better digital readability.
