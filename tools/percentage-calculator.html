How Percentage Calculations Work

Master every type of percentage calculation with these clear formulas

🎯

Percentage of a Number

Find X% of any value. Most common use: discounts, tax, tips.

Result = (X / 100) × Y

Example: 25% of 200 = 50

📈

Percentage Increase

How much has a value grown? Useful for salary hikes, price rises.

((New − Old) / Old) × 100

Example: 80→100 = 25% increase

📉

Percentage Decrease

How much has a value fallen? Great for sale discounts, depreciation.

((Old − New) / Old) × 100

Example: 100→80 = 20% decrease

↔️

Percentage Difference

Relative difference between two values without a defined direction.

|V1−V2| / ((V1+V2)/2) × 100

Example: 80 vs 100 = 22.2%

What Is a Percentage Calculator?

A percentage calculator is a free online tool that helps you compute percentages instantly without manual arithmetic. Whether you're a student solving homework problems, a professional analyzing data, or a shopper calculating discounts — our tool covers every scenario in seconds.

Types of Percentage Calculations

Our calculator handles four common percentage problems:

  • Percentage of a number — Find what X% of Y equals (e.g., 15% of ₹2,000 = ₹300)
  • Percentage increase — Calculate new value after a % rise (e.g., salary hike of 10%)
  • Percentage decrease — Calculate new value after a % fall (e.g., price drop of 30%)
  • Percentage difference — Compare two values to find how much they differ as a %

Real-World Examples

Here are everyday situations where our simple percentage calculator saves time:

  • 🛍️ Shopping: Product costs ₹1,500 with a 20% discount → you save ₹300, pay ₹1,200
  • 💼 Salary: Current CTC ₹6L, 12% hike → new CTC = ₹6.72L
  • 📊 Marks: Scored 85 out of 100 → that's 85%
